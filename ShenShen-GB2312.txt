**  corrections have been made.
**
**  The following symbols are used
**
**  1 (Following a character) most common pronunciation
**  2 (Following a character) second most common pronunciation
**  3 (Following a character) third most common pronunciation
**  4 (Following a character) fourth most common pronunciation
**  - (Following a character) Wendu (ÎÄ¶Á) pronunciation
**  = (Following a character) Baidu (°×¶Á) pronunciation
**  {}   Brackets comments
**  **   Starts a commented line
**  []   Indicates a compound character which has a unicode representation
**  [ ..#]  Indicates a compound character which has no unicode representation
**  #    Stands in place of a character which has no unicode representation
**  £¿   Stands in place of an unrepresentable character
**
**  Mistakes and omissions are believed to be few.
